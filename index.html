The Open Psychological Science Nigeria is a community of researchers dedicated to advancing transparent, reproducible, and open psychological research in Nigeria.

We meet for workshops, tutorials, and webinars to learn open science tools an principles. We also collaborate on metascience projects in psychological, social, and behavioural sciences in Nigeria.

We are currently supported by the generous time and efforts of our volunteers.
